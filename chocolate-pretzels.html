Chocolate pretzels

these chocolate pretzels are a quick and easy snack for any small gathering

ingredients

  • wax paper
  • pretzels of any style
  • chocolate
  • sprinkles

Steps

  1. lay out pretzels on wax paper.
  2. melt chocolate and pour over pretzels.
  3. add sprinkles if wanted.
  4. let dry and enjoy.
