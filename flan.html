flan

very tasty recipe for flan

ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cup sugar
  • 6 large eggs
  • 14oz condensed milk
  • 2 13oz evaporated milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Steps

  1. preheat oven to 325 F, heat a pot of water for the water bath. pour 1 cup of sugar into a warm pan over medium heat
  2. stir sugar until it browns and turns into caramel.
  3. immediately pour caramel into the molds you are using.
  4. whisk all of the eggs. Add the condensed milk and evaporated milk, mix well.
  5. slowly mix in the remaining 1/2 cup of sugar. Add the vanilla and mix thoroughly to make a silky mixture.
  6. pour mixture into caramel lined molds. place molds in large baking pan with tall sides and add hot water in the pan around molds for a depth of 2 inches.
  7. cover pan with foil and bake for 1 hour. carfully take molds out of hot water.
  8. let cool to room temperature, then chill in refrigerator for 1 hour while still in mold
  9. enjoy!
